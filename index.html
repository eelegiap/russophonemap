Map: Russian Language Speakers in Post-Soviet Countries

Paige Lee, GENED 1140, December 2022

The map below shows the proportion of the population in the region (typically a state or province) who are native Russian speakers (Russophones). The countries with data include Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Belarus, Ukraine, Moldova, Armenia, Georgia, and Kazakhstan. There were not sufficient data sources for Turkmenistan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, and Azerbaijan.
